About Us

Who We Are

The Rotaract Club of Butwal South is a dynamic community of young leaders in Butwal, Rupandehi, Nepal. As part of Rotaract International District 3292 (Nepal & Bhutan), we develop professional and leadership skills while emphasizing heartfelt service to our community.

With over two decades of active service, we have grown into one of the most vibrant Rotaract clubs in western Nepal. We proudly hosted the 17th Rotaract District Conference at the International Conference Center in Butwal, drawing more than 1,400 participants from across Nepal and Bhutan.

Our members come from diverse professional and academic backgrounds, united by the belief that young people are powerful agents of change — from cultural events like Teej Feast to hands-on service that touches lives every day.

Our Focus Areas

Four Avenues of Service

Like all Rotaract clubs, our work is organized around four core avenues that shape how we serve, grow, and lead together.

Avenue 01
🏘️
Community Service
Blood donation drives, health camps, clean-ups, and infrastructure projects for Butwal.
Avenue 02
💼
Professional Development
Workshops, career talks, and mentorship equipping members for meaningful careers.
Avenue 03
🌐
International Service
Participation in District 3292 events and global Rotaract initiatives worldwide.
Avenue 04
🎉
Club Service
Cultural events, Teej celebrations, charter nights, and NPL live screenings.
What We Do

Our Projects & Initiatives

From health camps in the hills to district-level conferences — our members take action that matters every single day.

🩸
Blood Donation Drives
Regularly organized camps connecting voluntary donors with patients in need across Rupandehi.
🏥
Health Camps
Free health screening including the Kamakhya Devi Slab Open initiative, reaching underserved communities.
📚
Education Support
Scholarships, stationery drives, and tutoring programs helping students from low-income families.
🌿
Environmental Action
Tree plantation, clean-up drives, and campaigns protecting Butwal’s rivers and green spaces.
🎭
Cultural Programs
Teej Feast celebrations, cultural performances, and events preserving Nepali heritage.
🏆
Youth Leadership
Conferences, panel discussions, and mentorship shaping Nepal’s next generation of leaders.
Landmark Achievement

17th Rotaract District Conference Host

Rotaract Club of Butwal South proudly hosted the 17th Rotaract District Conference at the International Conference Center in Butwal — a landmark two-day event drawing over 1,400 participants focused on youth leadership, entrepreneurship, and nation building.
